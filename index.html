Our Story

Our History

A family tradition, one plate at a time.

At El Sombrero, we bring the bold, vibrant flavors of Mexico to the heart of El Monte. Our kitchen prepares every dish with fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes, from sizzling fajitas and savory enchiladas to handcrafted tacos and hearty burritos.

Whether you’re joining us for a family dinner, a quick lunch, or ordering takeout, we are proud to serve delicious Mexican cuisine with friendly service and a welcoming atmosphere. At El Sombrero, every meal is made with care and tradition.
